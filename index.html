Welcome to the Resume Optimizer 🔍

This tool is designed to help you enhance your resume using smart keyword analysis and compliance framework alignment.

🎯 Why Keywords Matter

Modern recruiting systems (ATS) scan resumes for specific keywords that match job requirements. Missing critical keywords can cause your resume to be filtered out — even if you're qualified. This tool analyzes your uploaded resume and highlights important security-related keywords that could improve your visibility.

⚙️ Framework Selection

Selecting a security compliance framework (like NIST 800-53, ISO 27001, or SOC 2) tailors the keyword analysis to emphasize that framework's requirements. If you plan to apply for roles that reference specific standards, selecting the right framework helps optimize your resume to align with those expectations.

🚀 How to Use

  • 1. (Optional) Select a compliance framework from the dropdown.
  • 2. Upload your resume file (.docx, .pdf, or .txt).
  • 3. Click "Analyze Resume" to generate your personalized skill matrix and smart suggestions.
  • 4. Use the highlighted missing keywords and enhancement suggestions to update your resume strategically!
