Welcome to the Resume Optimizer 🔍

Optimize your resume with targeted keyword analysis. Pick your field, upload your document, and get instant feedback on how well your resume aligns with industry-specific lingo.

🎯 Why Keywords Matter

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) automatically scan resumes for exact keywords. By matching the right terms, you drastically increase your chances of passing that first automated filter.

🌐 Industry Selection

Choose Cyber Security, Web Development, or Finance to tailor the analysis to the jargon recruiters expect in each field.

🚀 How to Use

  1. Select one of the three supported industries.
  2. If you chose Cyber Security, pick a compliance framework (optional).
  3. Upload your resume (.docx, .pdf or .txt).
  4. Click “Analyze Resume” and review your personalized report:
    • Overall Health Score
    • Detailed Skill Domain Coverage
    • Missing Keywords & Enhancement Suggestions
